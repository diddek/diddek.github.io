Bibliography

This bibliography consist of two parts: sources and literature. The sources listed here are only the ones I used for my thesis, there is much more material on the harmony of the spheres (for example, Joscelin Godwin compiled two sourcebooks: Music, Mysticism and magic. A Sourcebook. London, Penguin group, 1986. and The Harmony of the Spheres. A Source book of the Pythagorean Tradition in Music. Rochester, Inner Trad. Int, 1993.).
