Introduction

The subject of this page is the Greek historian Herodian (third century AD).

Once, it was set up to become a site containing the results of my Ph.D. research, or, that is what I had in mind. But I've abandoned that project.

My Ph.D. research was to be a historical and philological commentary on the sixth book of Herodian’s History of the Empire from the Time of Marcus Aurelius: the life and reign of ‘that amiable prince’ (Gibbon), Alexander Severus (AD 222-235).

However, I did some work, and I might put up the bibliograpy here, as a Herodian bibliografy, other material (links, images, etc.).
